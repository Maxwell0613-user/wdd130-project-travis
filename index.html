    Purpose

    An online destination for lovers of cinnamon rolls and other baked goods! The idea is simple: have an online place to see my product, be able to do purchases and see reviews! Visitors will find product images, seasonal recipes, plus user-submitted photos and reviews. What makes us unique? We make everything fresh from scratch, no frozen ingredients or doughs! This is a business by my parents, but they didn't get very far so I wanted to continue the legacy and make it work because it's their passion and mine too!
