What Inspired Me?

I have always had a love for my father’s baking. He had taught me throughout the years how to bake, and it soon became one of my favorite hobbies. I soon became proficient in his secret cinnamon roll recipe considering that it was my favorite. Back when my father was newly married, they had the idea of making a business selling cinnamon rolls, but it didn’t succeed because the kitchen that they were going to begin in was shut down so they couldn’t start their dreams.

I decided to take upon myself to make their past efforts a reality and restart this business! So welcome here to Rocky Mountain Rolls and order yourself some delicious Cinnamon rolls!
